HISTORY

The Del Rey Deli Co is the brainchild of owner Tim Edwards and Corrina Murdy and Chef Vince Howard, and was established in 2013 to provide Playa Del Rey and the Westside with a source of fresh, high quality deli food. Sandwiches are our business, but so are fresh made soups, salads, great coffee and other goodies!
